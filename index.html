Writings

Master Thesis: Smooth Functorial Field Theory and the Geometric Cobordism Hypothesis [cite: 3]

This thesis used higher category theory to study quantum field theories via smooth bordism categories endowed with geometric data (e.g. Riemannian metrics)[cite: 3]. The main references for this thesis were the papers arXiv:2111.01095 and arXiv:2011.01208. [cite: 4]

[PDF]

Bachelor Thesis: Aspects of the theory of $C^{*}$-operator algebras [cite: 5]

This thesis delved into the theory of Banach and $C^{*}$ -algebras, including the Gel'fand transform and GNS construction, to ultimately prove the two Gel'fand-Naimark theorems, which represent $C^{*}$ -algebras as function algebras or operator algebras on Hilbert spaces[cite: 5].
