Writings

Master Thesis: Smooth Functorial Field Theory and the Geometric Cobordism Hypothesis

This thesis used higher category theory to study quantum field theories via smooth bordism categories endowed with geometric data (e.g. Riemannian metrics). The main references for this thesis were the papers arXiv:2111.01095 and arXiv:2011.01208.

[PDF]

Bachelor Thesis: Aspects of the theory of $C^{*}$-operator algebras

This thesis delved into the theory of Banach and $C^{*}$ -algebras, including the Gel'fand transform and GNS construction, to ultimately prove the two Gel'fand-Naimark theorems, which represent $C^{*}$ -algebras as function algebras or operator algebras on Hilbert spaces.
