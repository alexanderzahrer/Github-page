About Me

I am a mathematician from Austria. My academic interests revolve around:

  • Higher Category Theory
  • Higher Algebra
  • Derived/Spectral Algebraic Geometry
  • Stable Homotopy Theory
  • Functorial Field Theories

Seminars

Two friends of mine (Lucy Grossmann and Marcus Nicolas) and I enjoy organizing seminars on subjects we find particularly interesting. The following seminars have taken place or are currently ongoing:

  • Synthetic Seminar: A reading seminar focused on the upcoming book Formalization of Higher Categories by Denis-Charles Cisinski, Jakob Maximilian Cnossen, et al.
  • Derived Seminar: A seminar following the book A Study in Derived Algebraic Geometry by Dennis Gaitsgory and Nick Rozenblyum.

Writings

Master Thesis: Smooth Functorial Field Theory and the Geometric Cobordism Hypothesis

This thesis used higher category theory to study quantum field theories via smooth bordism categories endowed with geometric data (e.g. Riemannian metrics).

Bachelor Thesis: Aspects of the theory of $C^{*}$-operator algebras

This thesis delved into the theory of Banach and $C^{*}$ -algebras, including the Gel'fand transform and GNS construction, to ultimately prove the two Gel'fand-Naimark theorems.
